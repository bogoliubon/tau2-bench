   - File: src/tau2/scripts/human_agent_retail.py, line 485-497

     instead of subtracting refunds from payments.
   - Example: After modifying order #W5061109 (swapping speaker $302.67 -> $271.89),
     payment history shows Payment: $1319.43 and Refund: $30.78.
     Code computes Total as 1319.43 + 30.78 = $1350.21 (wrong).

   - Fix: Line 490, `total += amount` should check transaction_type and subtract for refunds.
   - Impact: Misleading total shown to human agent, caused incorrect total to be communicated to customer.
   - FIXED: commit 0e3dc09 — now subtracts refunds from total instead of adding them.
